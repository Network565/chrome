Bella Shmurda WHO IS BELLA SHMURDA

Bella Shmurda is a Nigerian hip-hop artiste and songwriter who is currently signed to Oneword Global Records label. Bella Shmurda’s real name is Akinbiyi Abiola Ahmed. Bella Shmurda hit stardom in the early part of 2019 and has continued to wow his fans with good music. Although widely known as Bella Shmurda, the young artiste also loves to refer to himself as ‘Fineboy Bella’.

The talented musician is signed under OnewordHQ and also strongly associated with Olamide’s Y.B.N.L record label. Bella Shmurda is a passion-driven artiste who hit publicity after releasing a remix of his debut single, ‘Vision 2020’ in 2019, wherein he featured Olamide. Bella Shmurda originally released the song in 2018, but it was not widely recognized until 2019, when he remixed it with Olamide and featured Poco Lee, an endowed Nigerian dancer, in the music video. Ever since Bella Shmurda hit fame, he has worked vigorously to improve his career. He has released several hit songs which featured top Nigerian musicians. One of them is Cash App which featured Zlatan Ibile. Bella Shmurda also got featured in Olamide’s recently released album, ‘Carpe Diem’, which took the internet by storm.
